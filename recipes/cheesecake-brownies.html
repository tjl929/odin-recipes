Chunky Cheesecake Brownies Recipe

Home

Description

Indulge in these irresistibly smooth brownies that are both simple and sophisticated. Rich chocolate brownies swirled with creamy cheesecake create delightful squares that everyone loves! Each bite offers the perfect balance of sweetness, making these brownies a crowd-pleaser at any gathering. Whether you're serving them at a party or enjoying them as a treat for yourself, these decadent squares never fail to impress.

They're as beautiful as they are delicious!


Prep Time:

25 minutes

Cook Time:

30 minutes

Servings:

16


Ingredients

*Note: To double the serving size, multiply each ingredient by 2.

  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease a 9-inch square baking pan.
  3. Combine cream cheese with 1/4 cup sugar and 1 egg in a mixing bowl; beat until smooth.
  4. Stir 1 cup chocolate chips into the cream cheese mixture. Set aside.
  5. Fill a saucepan with water and bring to a boil.
  6. Turn the heat off, and set a heatproof mixing bowl over the water.
  7. In the mixing bowl, combine butter with the remaining cup of chocolate chips; stir until just melted and blended together.
  8. Stir in the remaining 1/2 cup sugar and 2 eggs, then sift together flour, baking powder, and salt; stir into chocolate until evenly blended.
  9. Pour half of the batter into the prepared baking pan.
  10. Spread the cream cheese mixture over the chocolate layer.
  11. Top with remaining chocolate mixture (this doesn't need to completely cover the cream cheese layer).
  12. Using a knife, swirl the top chocolate layer into the cream cheese to make a marble pattern.
  13. Bake in preheated oven at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes, or until top is crinkled and edges pull away from sides of the pan. Cool thoroughly.
  14. Cut into 12 to 16 squares. Store in the refrigerator or freeze.
